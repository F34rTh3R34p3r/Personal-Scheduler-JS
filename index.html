Work Day Scheduler

A simple calendar app for scheduling your work day

Instructions: Fill your time-slots with important tasks.
Hit the 'lock' symbol on the right to save your tasks, even after refreshing.
You can always update what you've saved.
Erase everything by pressing the 'Clear Calendar' button at the bottom of the application.
